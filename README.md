📌 Overview
This project presents an end-to-end data analytics workflow in the banking domain, starting from data extraction to business insights and dashboard visualization.

The goal of this project is to analyze customer behavior, loans, deposits, and risk patterns to derive actionable business insights.

🔗 Project Workflow
. Data Extraction (SQL)
. Data sourced from MySQL (localhost)
. Queried and structured relevant banking datasets
. Data Analysis (Python - Jupyter Notebook)
. Data cleaning and preprocessing
. Handling missing values
. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
. Distribution analysis (income, loans, deposits)
. Correlation analysis between financial variables
. Data Visualization (Power BI)
. Built interactive dashboards
. Created KPIs and business metrics
. Used slicers for dynamic filtering
. Applied DAX for calculated measures

📊 Key Insights
. Total Loans (4.38bn) exceed Total Deposits (3.77bn) → potential liquidity risk
. Medium-income customers contribute the majority of loans and deposits
. Private Banking segment generates the highest value
. Business lending (2.60bn) dominates → higher exposure to risk
. Lower loyalty tiers (Jade) contribute more than premium customers → untapped opportunity
. Customer base is geographically concentrated, indicating diversification needs.

💡 Business Recommendations
. Improve deposit growth to balance loan exposure
. Target high-value customers with personalized offerings
. Diversify loan portfolio to reduce business lending risk
. Expand customer base across different regions
. Enhance cross-selling strategies for underutilized segments

🛠 Tools & Technologies
1. SQL (MySQL) – Data extraction
2. Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn) – Data analysis & EDA
3. Jupyter Notebook – Analysis environment
4. Power BI – Dashboard & visualization
5. DAX – Calculated measures.
